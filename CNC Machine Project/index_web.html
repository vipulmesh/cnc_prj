About Us

The High Speed Machining Centre is established to provide students with practical exposure to advanced manufacturing technologies. The cell focuses on training students in Computer Numerical Control (CNC) and Vertical Machining Center (VMC) operations, programming, and component manufacturing. Through hands-on workshops, industrial visits, and live projects, students gain valuable skills in precision machining, tool path planning, and automation. The club encourages innovation, teamwork, and technical excellence, preparing students for careers in modern manufacturing industries.
